Forgetting one's family members and friends due to an ailment would make a person mentally weak. Coming to the aid of such people was the biggest motivation !
It helps a person with Alzheimer's remember their family members and people close to him/her by presenting memory based questions in a form of a mini web game.
We built it using Javascript, HTML5 and CSS
Understanding the user's perspective at every phase of development since the target audience does not come under the regular category. The UI had to be designed in a way which would help them use the app with ease
That this app would be useful to develop a positive environment around someone who is mentally weak
Teamwork and new javascript frameworks
We intend on adding features which would include music and integrate Amazon Alexa that would give it a personal touch.
