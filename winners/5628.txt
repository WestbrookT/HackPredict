We were inspired by the Jarvis robot in Iron Man.
It enables you to control your house with your cell phone with voice commands.
With the help of the nuance api, Android studio, python and an arduino.
TCP communication between cell phone and computer, serial communication between computer and arduino, DDNS, Hardware
The whole project. It it a project that we were all interested it in and to be able to create a great first prototype is a exciting accomplishments.
Serial communication, app development, tcp communication, voice command, nuance api.
Expanding it to control not just our home but the things around us in the city.
