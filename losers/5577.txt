Alien Bounty Hunter is a 3D and 360 degree search and find adventure in the spirit of "Where's Waldo" designed for VR.  Search your surroundings in all directions to find your designated alien bounties.  Capturing a suspect means big rewards as well as information that will lead you to higher value targets on your quest to take down the ultimate alien crime boss.
Alien suspects and innocent civilians are drawn in a humorous cartoon style.  Characters are drawn at varying levels of depth for a fun 3D experience.  Characters and scenes are randomly constructed from an array of varying alien body parts, providing a unique experience every play-through.
