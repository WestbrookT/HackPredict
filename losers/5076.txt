Develop a system to monitor a farming land, quality of the soil, feed the required amount of water and monitoring the electricity usage. This information will be sent to the cloud. 
In the back end, we'll analyze the data and provide meaningful information to the users.
Farmers at the other end gets the real time information on their mobile phone through our application.
Also, this information can be shared with Government for further decisions of water usage and electricity usage of that area. Post which Government can supply the needed water and electricity.
We can also provide information related to productivity of the farming land.
