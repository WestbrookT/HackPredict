A perfect tool for pitching illustrations. Keep track of the scores of both individual and teams with SCOREO.
Key features:
-for 3-15 players per game
-multiple rounds possible for each game
-multiple judges
-idea attachments (all file-types supported!)
-player profiles
-judge notes
-easy-to-share - links for the audience
-versatile reporting: idea and player ranking with notes.
-easy-to-export (excel etc.)
