You play as an incredibly powerful superhero that has recently saved the city of Periliousville and due to the lack of bad guys you are now out of work and must find another way to pay the bills and help citizens. Missions include getting Mr. Phillips to a meeting on time, taking old Ms. Gable's laundry to the dry cleaners, and saving Professor Snowball from a tree. Despite this period of peace and prosperity, you know you must stay sharp, for it is only a matter of time until danger strikes again in Periliousville!
