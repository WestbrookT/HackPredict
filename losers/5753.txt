Hive - Pay 4 Get 5! 
Smart Software for Shopfans and Businesses. We help shoppers get great discounts without carrying member cards, as well as being able to pay contactless thanks to ibeacons. The Shoppers can also make offers to other buyers who just got the last sold favourite item. The Businsses don't need to issue anymore member cards and can offer great discounts by tracking each client's history of purchases.
