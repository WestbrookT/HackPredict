Z Race is is a racing game. Z Race is a zombie game. Z Race is a game to drive fast in a race while you kill and avoid zombies. If a racing game and a zombie fps game would have a baby it would be Z Race
Z Race takes place after the zombie plague. Humans get even using zombies for blood sports. Z Race is just one of them. So Drive and kill at Z Race!
Two modes:
-Quick Race: Race against other cars in a track full of zombies
-Drive and Kill: Kill zombies with your car or gun. No score. Just for fun!
Gamepad is required for the Quick Race Mode but not for Drive and Kill - you can just stay in the car and swipe your Gear VR to shoot zombies
After you get the loading message it takes 30 segs for the race to start
