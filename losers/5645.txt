It's basically Mine Cart Shooting Simulator and Virtual community Simulator.
It divided 2 parts and also has multy playing system.
In a 'Mine of Oberon' Part, 
you'll get some energy source from gemstone by shoot them
and use it for select some sort of stuffs that you like in entrance.
It'll be The Seed for creating random dreaming world.
Each stuffs has different Theme.
In a 'Dreaming world' Part,
you can travel and playing with your friends.
Dreaming world is created by stuffs that you choose from Mine of Oberon.
Every random map world will be disappear when you return to the Mine 
or exit the game and you'll never get into the same world.(like a dream.)
