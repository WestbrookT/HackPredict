Using telekinesis the player will manipulate objects around a facility slowly increasing their control and power. An event will occur which reveals the player to be the last hope of a failing military project. Once it has been determined that the project is a failure, all evidence must be destroyed to include, of course, the player. You must use your psionic power to escape certain death and the facility. Their will be many trying to stop you.
The power to lift objects and leave them suspended in air to provide shields against munitions.
From small to large, your telekinetic power will be able to manipulate most, if not all, objects.
Telekinesis in 1st person, movement through the level in 3rd person view.
Built for use with the touch-pad to mirror the effect of touching your fingers to your temple which symbolizes deep thoughts, and focus.
