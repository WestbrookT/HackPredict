5 individuals, one body and A detective with A mysterious power. Solve the mystery before the hunter gets hunted.
Murder Party is a Mystery Thriller Puzzle game about a detective who has the ability to see the ghosts of the dead, before they’ll actually die. Ray has a knack of solving strange and wacky cases thanks to his gift and his upcoming case is no different. Ray meets A ghost who asks for help to find their killer but when Ray arrives and meets the suspects he’s shocked to find all of them have ghosts.
The game will be very similar to Phoenix Wright and the Danganronpa series in that it will be 2 genres in 1.
Visual Novel storytelling and Puzzle like gameplay.
