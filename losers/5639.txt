The objective of each match is simple: Eliminate the opponent squad.
Both squads are controlled by AI and will automatically seek out and try to eliminate the opposition squad.
As the squad commander, players are able to switch between FPS VR view among the squad members.
Players are able to give basic commands to try and turn the tide of the battle. e.g. hold, advance
At the end of each match, players will earn upgrades to make their squad stronger.
There are also different stages to conquer in Squad Commander.
